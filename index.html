About this repository

Welcome to the official association of circulating intermediate markers or genetic polymorphisms with Mammographic Density(MMD). This repository is dedicated to hosting and showcasing interactive figures and visualizations derived from our analysis.

We employed interactive data visualization tools, primarily utilizing Plotly. We used Parcat plots, Treemaps, Sunburst plots, and other hierarchical plotting methods to interactively present the heterogeneous data we observed in Mammographic Density Literature. These plots simplify the depth of heterogeneity across studies and synthesize the results of the individual studies. The purpose was to present the direction of association between biomarkers or their related genetic polymorphism and MMD.

Project overview

This project aims to explore and elucidate the complex relationships between various intermediate biomarkers and MMD. The intermediate biomarkers included several biomarkers broadly categorized into three pathways: Sex steroids, Inflammation, and Insulin and Insulin-like Growth factors. Our systematic review encompasses a wide range of data on the three pathways. We analyzed data extracted from individual studies and presented a comprehensive view of the existing evidence.
